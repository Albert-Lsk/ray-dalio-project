Ray Dalio is highly successful investor, hedge fund manager, author, and billionaire. He founded the American investment firm Bridgewater Associates and authored multiple New York Times bestselling books like, ‘Principles: Life and Work’, and ‘Principles For Dealing With a Changing World’.

Having started from his firm out of his two-bedroom apartment back in 1975, to then losing everything just 7 years later and being so broke he had to borrow $4,000 loan from his father to rebuild; Ray Dalio bounced back from rock-bottom to become a multibillionaire who’s firm now manages over $123.5 billion in assets. As such, we’re publishing some of the best Ray Dalio quotes to help you peer into a mind that knows how to overcome failure and setbacks, and thrive in a changing world.

So, if you’re ready for some true words of wisdom, then let’s dive into these powerful Ray Dalio quotes:

10. “By and large, life will give you what you deserve.” – Ray Dalio

11. “The greatest gift you can give someone is the power to be successful. Giving people the opportunity to struggle rather than giving them the things they are struggling for will make them stronger.” – Ray Dalio

27. “Whatever success I’ve had has been more due to knowing how to deal with my not knowing than anything I know.” – Ray Dalio
